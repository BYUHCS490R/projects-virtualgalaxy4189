Ekko

Ekko is one of the youngest kids in the orphan group that Vander looks after and considers his kids. Ekko has a signature look of darker skin with white hair. He has a rebellious and mischevious character but is extremely reliable when needed. His nickname is the "Boy Savior" and he lives up to that nickname. He also has a tragic romance story with the mentally unstable Powder/Jinx.

- Teenage prodigy
He is incredibly smart at a very young age
- Genius inventor
He is capable of creating extraordinary devices with limited resources and even more incredible things with sufficient resources
- Leader of the Firelights
The Firelights are a kind of vigilanty group in the underworld, not aligned with Piltover but not aligned with Zaun either. they find and crae for the people affected by the war between Piltover and Zaun.
