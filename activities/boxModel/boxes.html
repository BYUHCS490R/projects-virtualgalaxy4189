Box Model

Engagement

  1. Block tags create boxes and inline tags just select text with no line breaks

  2. The margin is the space outside the box. The padding is the space between the box and the stuff inside of it. The border is the outline of the box

  3. Pixels, centimetres, inches, percentage, em and rem

  4. You use a figure tag when you want to change an img tag into a block tag
