The Fast & Furious movie series is a high-energy action franchise centered around street racing, crime, and a strong theme of family. The story mainly follows Vin Diesel as Dominic Toretto and Paul Walker as Brian O'Conner, along with a team of skilled drivers who become involved in dangerous missions around the world.
